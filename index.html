Our Story

HOME FOOD was born from the culinary passion of Mrs.Sudesh Devi , a homemaker with a dream. For years, Sudesh ji's delicious home-cooked meals were the talk of her neighborhood in Delhi. Encouraged by her family and friends, she decided to turn her love for cooking into a business venture.

Starting with a small kitchen in her home, Sudesh ji's authentic vegetarian dishes quickly gained popularity. Today, HOME FOOD has grown into a beloved chain of restaurants, bringing the warmth and flavors of home-cooked Indian meals to food lovers across multiple cities. Every dish we serve is a testament to Sudesh ji's commitment to quality, taste, and the joy of sharing good food.
